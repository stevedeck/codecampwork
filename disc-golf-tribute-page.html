Disc Golf Is Awesome!

Disc Golf is an accessible game for all ages

  • An early version of disc golf was played in Canada where friends would throw a frisbee into a bucket
  • Modern disc golf has roots across several states in the 1960s
  • The game is simple - throw the frisbee/disc into a basket. The number of throws is your score. Lowest score wins!

Have fun with your family and play disc golf at your local course (they're often free!)
